## Key Topics

- Eucalyptus poles: quantities by diameter and length
- Gabion materials: wire cages, geotextile, stone fill, drainage pipe
- Insulation: straw bales, clay, burlap mesh
- Waterproofing: EPDM membrane, seam tape, fasteners
- Finishes: lime, sand, earth pigments
- Openings: door and window units, hardware
- Solar system: panels, inverter, batteries, wiring
- Living roof: substrate mix, plants
