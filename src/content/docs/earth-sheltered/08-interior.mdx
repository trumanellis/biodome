## Key Topics

- Limewash: white or earth-pigmented, three coats for breathable finish
- Floor options: rammed earth, concrete slab, stone pavers
- Thermal mass floor: dark-colored surface to absorb solar gain from south windows
- Built-in furniture: eucalyptus pole benches, shelving, loft platform
- Moisture management: passive venting, humidity-buffering lime finish
